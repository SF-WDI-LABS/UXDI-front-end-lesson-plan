UXDI Is A Great Class

Being a UXDI student at General Assembly is a great way to grow some of the skills you use most as a UX Designer. Some of the most important skills you can learn here include:

  • Design thinking
  • Iterative approaches to UX
  • The difference between UX and UI
  • How to work with developers
  • Web development fundamentals
